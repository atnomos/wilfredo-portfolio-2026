About

Wilfredo Valle

Portrait Photographer  ·  San Francisco

I photograph people the way you remember them — not posed, not performed, but present. My portraits are quiet studies in character: the weight behind a uniform, the calm before a performance, the warmth in someone who has spent their career in service to others.

My path to photography runs through design and storytelling. A BFA from the Academy of Art University gave me the compositional foundation; years as a Creative Trainer at Apple sharpened my eye for what makes a moment connect. Today I work as a Technical Program Manager at Disney, where I think every day about how stories move people — and I bring that same intentionality to every portrait I make.

I believe a great portrait is a collaboration. My background in design and program management means I come to every shoot with both a clear visual vision and the structure to put people at ease — so the person in front of the lens can let go and simply be themselves. That's the moment I'm after.
